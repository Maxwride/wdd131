WDD 131-[section]

This is a site where people can come to get a solid pickleball paddle. It will also help them choose between spin or power paddles

Purpose

Our purpose is to help the boyz select a picklebaddle according to these three catagories spin,power, and value. Alot of people arent sure what paddle to get based on the type of player that they are this website will fix this issue.

Audience

People that are more into pickleball than the average person.

Dynamic elements

My child page will dynamically bring in the images and the user will be able to choose a category based on a drop down menu. The descriptions for each paddle will change according to the sort.
